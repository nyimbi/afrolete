Based on a thorough review of this comprehensive PRD, here are the **features, capabilities, and functionality that appear to be missing or underdeveloped**:

---

### 1. **Operational & Logistical Gaps**
- **Volunteer Management**: Clubs and schools rely heavily on volunteers (coaches, drivers, event staff). No system for recruiting, scheduling, training, or acknowledging volunteers.
- **Equipment & Inventory Management**: Tracking sports equipment, uniforms, maintenance schedules, check-in/check-out, and depreciation.
- **Facility & Venue Management**: Booking systems for fields, courts, pools; maintenance logs; availability calendars; conflict resolution.
- **Transportation & Logistics**: Team travel planning, bus scheduling, permission for away games, tracking arrival/departure.
- **Merchandise & E-commerce**: Club/school merchandise stores integrated into the platform (jerseys, gear, tickets).
- **Meal & Nutrition Planning**: For camps, tournaments, or high-performance programs—meal tracking, dietary plans, allergy alerts.

---

### 2. **Engagement & Community Features**
- **Social Feed / Wall**: A team or club social feed for sharing moments, photos, wins, announcements—like a private social network.
- **Fan Engagement Tools**: For clubs that want to engage supporters—ticketing, fan zones, live chat during streams, fan polls.
- **Alumni Network**: Tracking former players, engagement tools, reunions, mentorship programs, fundraising.
- **Sponsorship Activation Tools**: Beyond exposure metrics—sponsor content upload, branded challenges, coupon codes, sponsor dashboards.
- **Fundraising & Donation Management**: Campaign tracking, donor management, integration with platforms like GoFundMe.

---

### 3. **Technical & Deployment Considerations**
- **Offline-First Functionality**: For areas with poor connectivity (common in some regions)—ability to record data offline and sync later.
- **Data Portability & Export Standards**: Support for exporting data in standard formats for third-party analysis (e.g., CSV for Excel, JSON for APIs).
- **White-Labeling & Custom Branding**: For larger organizations that want their own branded app/portal.
- **Low-Bandwidth Mode**: Optimized for slower networks—reduced image quality, text-only mode.
- **Hardware Integration Kits**: Recommended hardware bundles (cameras, GPS devices) with setup guides and pre-configured integrations.

---

### 4. **AI & Data Enhancements**
- **Opposition Scouting & Analysis Tools**: Upload opponent match videos, generate automated scout reports, tactical weaknesses detection.
- **Predictive Injury Modeling**: More advanced than workload ratios—incorporating weather, playing surface, fatigue biomarkers.
- **Career & Pathway Projection**: For youth athletes—AI that projects potential pathways (college, semi-pro, pro) based on performance trends.
- **Voice Commands for Coaches**: “Hey AfroLete, log a goal for Emma” during a match via smartwatch or headset.
- **Automated Highlight Reel Creation**: AI that creates personalized highlight reels for players/parents/scouts.

---

### 5. **Financial & Administrative Tools**
- **Scholarship & Financial Aid Management**: Application, approval, and tracking of athletic scholarships or fee reductions.
- **Budgeting & Forecasting Tools**: For club administrators—projected costs, revenue forecasting, cash flow analysis.
- **Grant Management**: Tracking grant applications, deadlines, reporting requirements.
- **Insurance Claim Integration**: Link to insurance providers for injury claims, equipment loss/damage.

---

### 6. **Player Development & Wellness**
- **Mental Health & Wellness Tracking**: Mood logging, stress levels, access to sports psychologists, mental resilience exercises.
- **Academic Integration & Monitoring**: For student-athletes—grade tracking, study hour logging, eligibility alerts.
- **Life Skills & Career Development Modules**: Content on leadership, nutrition, media training, financial literacy.
- **Dual Career Support**: For athletes balancing sport with education or work—scheduling support, time management tools.

---

### 7. **Compliance & Safety Enhancements**
- **Background Check Integration**: Automated checks for coaches/staff with renewal reminders.
- **Incident Reporting & Management**: Formal incident logging (injuries, misconduct, safeguarding concerns) with workflow for resolution.
- **Weather Integration & Alerts**: Real-time weather alerts for outdoor events, heat index warnings, lightning detection.
- **Eligibility & Transfer Certificate Management**: Digital transfer certificates, clearance status tracking between clubs/countries.

---

### 8. **Competition & Event Enhancements**
- **Ticketing & Access Control**: Digital tickets, QR code scanning at gates, attendance tracking.
- **Broadcast & Media Management**: Tools for live streaming integration, commentator feeds, post-match interview scheduling.
- **Awards & Ceremony Management**: Nominations, voting, digital award certificates, ceremony planning.
- **Multi-Sport Event Support** (e.g., Olympics-style events): Complex scheduling across multiple venues and sports.

---

### 9. **Internationalization & Localization**
- **Regional Rule Adaptations**: Sport rules vary by country/region—system should adapt metrics and compliance accordingly.
- **Local Payment Methods**: M-Pesa, PayPal, local bank transfers, cash payment tracking.
- **Government & Federation Reporting**: Automated report generation for national sports bodies, ministry of education, etc.
- **Local Language Support Beyond UI**: AI models trained on local languages for audio processing.

---

### 10. **Long-Term & Scalability Features**
- **Archival & Legacy Data Management**: Long-term storage solutions for historical data, GDPR-compliant archiving.
- **API Marketplace**: Where third-party developers can build and sell extensions (e.g., specialized analytics, custom reports).
- **Disaster Recovery & Data Migration Services**: Offered as a paid service for clubs moving from other systems.
- **Community Templates & Best Practices**: Pre-built templates for drills, training plans, consent forms shared across the platform.

---

### 11. **User Experience & Onboarding**
- **Interactive Tutorials & Coach Education**: In-app walkthroughs, coaching certification courses.
- **Augmented Reality (AR) Features**: AR overlays for training drills, stadium navigation, equipment assembly.
- **Voice Feedback for Players**: AI that gives real-time audio feedback during training via bone conduction headphones.
- **Family Accounts**: One parent account managing multiple children across different sports/teams.

---

### 12. **Ethical & Governance Features**
- **Algorithmic Bias Detection & Reporting**: Tools to audit AI recommendations for bias (gender, age, ethnicity).
- **Data Donation & Research Opt-in**: Players can donate anonymized data for sports science research.
- **Ethical AI Transparency Reports**: Regular reports on how AI models are trained and used.

---

### Summary Recommendation:
The PRD is **exceptionally strong** in core AI/analytics, performance tracking, and technical architecture. The biggest gaps are in **operational logistics** (equipment, volunteers, facilities), **community and engagement tools**, **financial management**, and **real-world deployment considerations** (offline mode, low-bandwidth support).

Many of these missing features represent opportunities for **Phase 6+ development** or could be addressed through the **API ecosystem** and third-party integrations.
